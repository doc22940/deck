Deck

'Deck' is a multi-timeline viewer for GitHub repository events.
It is also supported for Backlog produced by Nulab Inc.
It is made of responsive design for PC or mobile modern browsers.

Get Stared Now See a Demo

All your account information are saved in local storage of the browser.
Your data is not saved on server-side at all.
Setting import/export is supported so as to move yours to another browser.
